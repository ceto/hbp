Tour details

Discover the medieval Old Town of Buda, and enjoy the panoramic view from the Fisherman’s Bastion! Visit the today catholic Matthias Church, which was transformed to be a mosque during the Ottoman era. Did you know that there is a statue of Mary, which was preserved by the turks?

Take a walk around the Royal Palace enjoy the panoramic view on the Danube and learn about the history of the Ottoman occupation. Discover the hidden history and the relics of the Muslim rule in the Buda Castle.

Finally cross the Danube to visit the Inner City Parish Church which was also a mosque in the medieval time and its mithrab where the name of Allah is beautifully preserved.
