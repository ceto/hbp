Don't miss the highlights

With the help of our experienced guide, discover the jewels of our city, including two UNESCO World Heritage areas. We have a few recommendations:

  • Enjoy a panoramic view of the city from Gellért Hill and take a walk around the Citadel to see the Statue of Liberty
  • In the Buda Castle see the Royal Palace and Matthias Church, which was operating as a mosque during the Ottoman era.
  • Visit the Parliament of Hungary, Liberty Square and St. Stephen’s Basilica.
  • Hop on the oldest subway of the continent to discover Andrássy Avenue, Heroes’ Square, the Opera House and City Park.

Who Are We?

We are Muslims living in Hungary. We would like to encourage everybody to visit our beautiful city and listen to our history related to Islam.


Muslim walks for locals

We give Muslim Walk for locals to get to know the basics of Islam. We take schools and clubs and friends for a walk Our Muslim Walk was visited by Reuters and Euronews.

Halal tours visitors

And we give halal tours for our visitors. If you decide to come to Hungary we organize your holiday - airport transfers, walking or bus tours and everything you wish in a costumized way.

Islamic sights of Budapest

Our guide can give you an insight into the daily life of Hungarian Muslims today. We provide special programs that you can't find in a travel guide.

  • With our guide’s help you can visit our local masjids, including the Budapest Mosque, the largest Islamic Center of Eastern Europe, the oldest apartment mosque of Budapest, and many more fascinating sites.
  • Discover the Ottoman Heritage of the Buda Castle: the Monument of Abdurrahman Pasha, and much more.
  • Visit the freshly renovated Tomb of Gül Baba and some of the Turkish Baths that are still operating in the city
