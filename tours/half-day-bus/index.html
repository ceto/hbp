Tour details

Let us drive up to Gellert Hill to enjoy the most beautiful river view of Europe. Take a walk to the former Liberation Monument and the fortress of Citadel - It will be more comfortable by minivan. Then pass by the Cave Church and hear about ancient Muslim tribes in Europe from the era of migration.

Drive around in the Buda Castle District to see the the monuments of the former medieval capital of Hungary. Then cross the river on the famous Chain Bridge! On the Pest side visit Andrássy avenue and Heroes’ Square with its statues of the Hungarian leaders throughout the ages. And visit the stunning Vajdahunyad Castle in the City Park.
