Tour details

We are driving up to Gellert Hill to enjoy the most beautiful river view of Europe. Take a walk to the former Liberation Monument and the Citadel (former fortress) - It might be more comfortable by minivan.

Visit the lobby of the famous building of Gellért Spa and Hotel. Visit the Cave Church and here about ancient Muslim tribes in Europe from the era of migration.

Cross the river on the famous Chain Bridge! On the Pest side visit Andrássy avenue and Heroes’ Square with its statues of the Hungarian leaders throughout the ages.
